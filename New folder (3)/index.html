ABOUT US

Background and Motivation
As concerns mount over social isolation, particularly for older adults vulnerable to retirement, loss, and mobility challenges, this data dashboard dives into trends of single-person households and self-reported loneliness. By exploring these factors, we hope to illuminate potential causes and empower individuals and communities with tools to foster stronger social connections, ultimately promoting the well-being of our aging population.
Objectives
1. To what extent are older adults experiencing loneliness? (Data on self-reported loneliness)
2. Is there a correlation between the rise of single-person households and reported loneliness, particularly among older adults? (Comparing data on single-person households and self-reported loneliness)
3. What insights can be gleaned from this data to understand the factors contributing to loneliness among older adults? (Analysis of combined data)
