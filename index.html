Background and Motivation
World weather visualization is a project aimed at graphically representing weather data from different regions around the globe. This type of visualization is crucial as it allows for a better understanding of weather patterns, trends, and anomalies. Weather data visualization can be used for various purposes, including climate research, disaster management, agriculture, and public information dissemination.

5. How are different weather parameters distributed across various regions or continents?
6. How do weather patterns evolve over time in different regions?
